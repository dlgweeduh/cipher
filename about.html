a little about why cipher exists

you have the drive, ability, and smarts to be a changemaker. we just want to help make it happen.

learning to code is a lot like learning how to play an instrument, how to swim, or how to shoot-a-dirty three-pointer: it's gotta be done with peers, in a healthy environment. when you're in a cipher circle, you progress together with your friends and with the help of some code-rockstar-mentors from your community.
